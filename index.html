Meet Chine Music & Film

Dedicated to producing, promoting and retaining Chicago's best music talent.

  • Who is Chine Music

    Founded in 2004 by Johnnie Coleman, Chine Music, originally known as "Time 2 Chine," was established to further a Hip Hop and R&B movement that was swelling within the city of Chicago. Johnnie, a successful entrepreneur and owner of three successful barber salons in downtown Chicago, recognized the potential of the artists who frequented his barber salons and the music that he and his customers listened to. Knowing this, they needed support and a platform to grow. Although mainstream Hip Hop recognized Chicago as an important locale and occasionally signed an artist or two, the industry's appreciation of Chicago music was not reflected in the distribution of record deals. Consequently, Chicago remained largely an untapped talent source. Capitalizing on the industry's snub, Johnnie formed the fledging label and immediately nurtured connections within the powerhouse that was Chicago music — the streets. He opened a downtown Chicago recording studio and began working with some of the most respected artists in the city - Bump J, and Sly Polaroid — and supported them in their efforts to bring Chicago Hip Hop to a place of prominence within the industry.

    A natural in identifying talent within the city, Johnnie has continued to keep his fingers on the pulse of the city and has played a key role in the development of new talent — most notably Yung Stakks, KeishKeish, Z-Money, D-Low and S.B.E. (StarBoyEra) — writing, producing and placing Chine Music at the forefront of the Midwest music scene. Chine Music's accomplishments are many; numerous records spinning on major radio, millions of video views, multiple successful mix tapes and singles distributed with various artists receiving national exposure, also playing an integral role in several artists being signed to major recording contracts. Much of this furthered through strong partnerships with music giants such as John Monopoly of Hustle., E-Class of PoeBoy Music Group, Toi Mapp of Hear The Siren, George and Regina Daniels, of Daniels Entertainment to name a few to usher Chine Music and the movement to the next level. Looking forward, Chine Music has its eye set on conquering much more within the field of entertainment — television, film, and merchandising — and with its track record, success is definitely on the horizon.
